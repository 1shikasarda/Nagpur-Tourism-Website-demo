-Ganesh Mandir, Tekdi-

Ganpati in the cities of Nagpur is also known as Ganapati of the hill. Shri Ganesha Temple is one of the ancient temples in the city of Nagpur. It is said about this temple that this temple has come into existence by itself. This temple is 250 years old and lakhs of devotees visit this temple.
Among the major religious tourist places in the cities of Nagpur, Shri Ganesha Mandir i.e. Ganesha of the Hill is a favorite place for tourists as well as devotees. Shri Ganesha Temple is located at Sitabuldi in the city of Nagpur.
