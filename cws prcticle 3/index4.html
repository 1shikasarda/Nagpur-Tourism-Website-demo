-Lata Mangeshkar Musical Garden-

Lata Mangeshkar Musical Garden is one of the famous gardens in the city of Nagpur. Lata Mangeshkar Musical Garden is one of the most peaceful and famous places in the city of Nagpur. Lata Mangeshkar Musical Garden is known as one of the best places to visit for kids and tourists in the city of Nagpur.
Lata Mangeshkar Musical Garden is a great place for an evening. Many tourists in the city of Nagpur prefer to visit this garden in the evening.This is a park located at a distance of eight kilometers from the city of Nagpur. Seeing the increasing number of tourists to this park, Nagpur became famous as the best tourist destination in the city. Lata Mangeshkar Musical Garden in Nagpur City is located at Subhash Road Empress City Nagpur opposite Gandhisagar.
